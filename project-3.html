Project 03

Desk with Integrated Charging Ports

CourseAPS 112 — Engineering Strategies & Practices II
RoleTeam Leader
Tags
Team LeadershipEngineering DrawingsMicrosoft ProjectGantt Chart

Overview

For my design course APS 112, my team and I were tasked with solving the problem of limited accessible power outlets for elementary school students. Students were frequently crowding around existing outlets and tripping over extension cords, creating safety hazards in the classroom.

Solution

  • Designed a student desk with two integrated charging port modules, each housing two standard wall adapters and one USB-A port — providing four wall outlets and two USB-A ports per desk.
  • Produced concentric engineering drawings of the desk and detailed dimensional drawings of the charging port module.
  • The charging port cutout was designed to precise millimetre tolerances to ensure a flush, secure fit within the desk surface.

My Role as Team Leader
